Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish composed of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and an array of cheeses, typically ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Baked to perfection, the dish emerges from the oven with a golden, bubbly crust, exuding a savory aroma that invites hearty appetites. Its flavorful, comforting taste makes it a popular choice for family gatherings and festive occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage (or a combination)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauc
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley or 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles (cooked according to package instructions)
  • 3-4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps to make the recipe

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the ground beef or sausage to the skillet. Cook until browned, breaking up the meat as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Add the sugar, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  7. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Place 3-4 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  10. Top with 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  11. Repeat layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella) two more times, ending with a layer of noodles and remaining meat sauce on top.
  12. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  13. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
  15. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired. Enjoy!
